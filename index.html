Alarm Configuration

This page shows the alarm configuration, i.e. which trigger PVs have been configured, and what guidance and display links are provided for each one.

The information is based on a daily snapshot of the alarm server. For the most recent detail and for live alarm state, i.e. which PVs are in alarm etc. refer to the IHC.

The 'Guidance' and 'Displays' counts on each PV reflect the total number guidance and display entries for a PV, combining all entries on the PV itself and those inherited from the hierarchical configuration.
